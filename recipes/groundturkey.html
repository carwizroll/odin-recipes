Ingredients

  • 93%/7% Lean Ground Turkey
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Salsa
  • Tortillas & Garnishes

Steps

  1. Mix the ground turkey and the seasonings
  2. Brown the meat on the stove
  3. Let the meat simmer on low in the salsa
  4. Load the tortillas and dress with garnish
